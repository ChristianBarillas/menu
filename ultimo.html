Blog entry 4 - final entry - period 3

Hey everyone! Can you believe it? We've reached the end of the English Didactics II blog saga – the grand finale! What a wild ride of wisdom and wittiness we've had together.

So, here's the scoop on the last class shindig, even though I missed it because I got roped into being an MU interpreter. Life, right? But fear not, because Maru spilled the tea in her blog, and I'm here to share the deets.

First up, Diana's gang rolled in with the classics – a quick recap and a round of Hangman. They even dropped some time bomb phrases like "a quarter to____" on us. Like, who knew telling time could be this cool? Then they dove into the 'there is' and 'there are' antics, wrapping things up with a writing extravaganza. And oh boy, the battle of wits between Group 1 and Group 2 brought the house down! Students were vibing with the whole showdown.

In the middle of the action, Teacher Orlando pulled off a superhero move – cookies and coffee for everyone! And get this, even the folks who missed the party got their share on Friday during French class. Now, that's some next-level sweetness.

Then, Eli's crew took the stage, bringing the razzle-dazzle with realia, props, and a clock that talked! Like, a clock that actually chatted away the hours. Mind-blown! Eli sprinkled some magic vocabulary dust – "timeless," "time flies," "in a jiffy," and whatnot. They dived into a reading gig and wrapped it all up with a basket of mystery objects. It was like a language carnival, and I loved every bit of it.

Big shoutout to our rockstar teacher! Your passion is contagious, and we all know there's a whole universe of effort behind those class hours. Every piece of advice was like gold – more than just theory, you made it real.

And to my awesome classmates, high fives all around! We brought our A-game, learned tons, and made this whole shebang memorable. As we close this chapter, I'm feeling the vibes of gratitude for the lessons and the excitement for the adventures waiting around the corner. Cheers to us!
