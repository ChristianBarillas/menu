Curious about PPP? Well, PPP stands for Presentation, Practice, and Production. These three phases are like the secret recipe for crafting an effective lesson plan. They're like signposts that guide teachers through the journey of organizing a class while also keeping an eye on how students are soaking up knowledge. Think of Presentation as the spotlight moment. This is where I, as the teacher, take center stage and unwrap the topic of the day. I've got a whole toolkit of resources at my disposal to make sure the students not only get it but also find it intriguing. Practice is where the action really kicks in.
