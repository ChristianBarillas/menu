Maria, in her role, briefly elucidated numbers and conceived a math-oriented activity to allow students to practice working with numbers. Nathaly introduced an enthralling activity named "Journalists and Celebrities," wherein students enacted roles and inquired about each other's phone numbers and ages. Remarkably, even with a total of six students, undivided attention and effective collaboration prevailed throughout the lesson.

Shifting the focus to Group 8, they continued their exploration of classroom objects within the same context.
