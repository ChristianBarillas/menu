PDP (Pre, During, and Post)



This past week, our classroom transformed into a dynamic theater of education, with the spotlight shining on the enchanting PDP method â€“ Presentation, Development, and Practice. We embarked on an odyssey of learning that leaned heavily on the twin pillars of reading and listening, embellished with a tapestry of imaginative activities designed to nurture our students' receptive prowess. To unravel the mystique of this methodology, our erudite guide chose a thematic compass: the captivating world of Holistic Medicine, a North Star guiding our pedagogical voyage.

Preparatory Overture: In this opening act, our teacher unveiled the parchment of wisdom, the thematic passage, a sacred scroll students needed to possess. The teacher, akin to a sage, emphasized the bestowal of this passage upon our students as an educational boon. Alongside this, the treasury of vocabulary was distributed among the eager minds, initiating an intellectual journey through a carefully choreographed activity.

Midway Flourish: Once our students had ingested the essence of our central theme, the symphony of learning moved to its interactive phase. Questions pirouetted through the air, and vibrant activities orchestrated a grand ballet of social exchange within our hallowed classroom. The active engagement of our students became the soulful rhythm of this educational waltz. These activities and inquiries were the threads that intricately wove together with the passage's narrative.

Climactic Crescendo: As we ascended to the zenith of our educational masterpiece, our mission crystallized: to empower our students with the gift of articulate discourse and a profound understanding of the enigmatic realm of Holistic Medicine. At this juncture, the canvas of creativity expanded, and with the luxury of time, we introduced more captivating activities. Here, the teacher became the artist, free to paint with the same thematic colors or explore entirely new horizons. Above all, we celebrated the nurturing of diverse learning skills.
