The first english class!

Hello everyone! I'm excited to share some great news with you. Last week, we officially kicked off our English classes, and it's been quite an exciting journey so far! We have 18 students joining us from last Thursday until the second week of November. In this course, they'll be diving into vocabulary and grammar through engaging activities prepared by each instructor group.

Diana's group had the privilege of being the first to lead a session. They focused on the essentials: the alphabet, introducing oneself, and basic greetings. To break the ice, they organized a fun chain activity where each student had to introduce themselves and remember the name of the person who spoke before them. For example, "Hello, my name is Elizabeth," and then the next person would introduce Elizabeth and share their own name, creating a chain of introductions that continued until the last person, who had the impressive task of remembering everyone's name.

What stood out was the collaborative effort of all our teachers during the lesson presentation. Each instructor shared their expertise, some delving into the nuances of alphabet sounds, while others focused on teaching various forms of greetings. This collective approach not only enriched the learning experience but also reflected our unwavering commitment to providing the best education.

In addition to these engaging activities, students had the chance to select a letter of the alphabet and come up with a word that starts with it. They also sharpened their skills by completing sentences from a dialogue. Our English classes are shaping up to be an exciting and enriching adventure, and we can't wait to witness the growth and progress of our talented students. Stay tuned for more updates and achievements as we continue this remarkable journey together!
